About Us

Step into Supreme Gyros, your go-to family-owned food truck, where a rich tradition of authentic flavors meets the warmth of our welcoming family. Proudly 100% halal, we invite you to savor the essence of our culinary heritage in every bite.

Our Business

Our main spot is at 671 Colusa Avenue! Keep an eye out, though, as we love to spice things up by exploring new locations from time to time. Follow us on social media for the latest updates on our mobile culinary adventures and discover where Supreme Gyros will be rolling into next!

At Supreme Gyros, we're more than a food truck; we're passionate culinary enthusiasts dedicated to redefining the gyro experience. With a commitment to exceptional flavors and quality, we bring the authentic taste of gyros to the streets, making it accessible for all who crave a mouthwatering and satisfying meal on the go.

What sets us apart is our dedication to creating a fusion of traditional recipes and innovative twists. Every ingredient is carefully selected to ensure a symphony of flavors in every bite. From our signature gyro pitas to our indulgent fries and platters, Supreme Gyros is a celebration of taste and a testament to the belief that great food has the power to bring people together.
